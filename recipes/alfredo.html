Chicken Alfredo

Description

Want a delicious, restaurant-style meal that is simple and easy to make? Look no further, as this chicken alfredo recipe has you covered. On top of being a delicious Itailian-inspired dish, this meal is also quick and should take no more than 15-20 minutes with a little experience.

Ingredients

  • one chicken breast
  • fettucine noodles
  • alfredo sauce
  • cayenne pepper powder
  • two eggs
  • flour
  • bread crumbs

Steps

  1. Pound chicken breast until tender
  2. Cut chicken breast into two cutlets
  3. Coat cutlets in flour, followed by eggs and bread crumbs
  4. Boil noodles in pot until soft
  5. Put chicken cutlets in air fryer, cook at 390 degrees for seven minutes
  6. While the chicken is cooking, pour the alfredo sauce into a pan and let it simmer
  7. After seven minutes, flip the chicken and cook for seven more minutes
  8. Put the noodles on a plate, take the chicken out, and pour the sauce on your dish
  9. Enjoy!
