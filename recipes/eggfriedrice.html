Egg Fried Rice

Description

Egg fried rice is one of the oldest and most simple recipes in the world, being renowned for it's simplicity, cost, and most importantly, flavor.

Ingredients

  • two eggs
  • jasmine rice
  • diced vegetables
  • soy sauce
  • chopped green onions

Steps

  1. scramble lightly salted eggs in pan until 3/4 cooked
  2. Pour jasmine rice on top and scramble in with eggs
  3. put diced vegetables in as well, but be careful not to put too much
  4. Once closer to being fully cooked, pour soy sauce around the edges of the pan and mix in
  5. put onto a plate and add some chopped green onions on top
  6. Enjoy!
