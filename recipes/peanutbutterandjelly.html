Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Description

A classic meal in busy American households, the peanut butter and jelly sandwich is a staple of many people's childhood. Easy to learn but difficult to master, this meal offers a surprising amount of variation

Ingredients

  • peanut butter (creamy or crunchy)
  • jelly (preferably grape)
  • two slices of white or honey roasted bread

Steps

  1. Spread peanut butter on one slice of bread
  2. spread jelly on the other slice of bread
  3. put one on top of the other, with both ingredients in the middle
  4. Make a cut down the middle
  5. Enjoy!
If you want you can do a lot to add variety such as remove the crust, grill it on a pan, add a third layer, etc.
